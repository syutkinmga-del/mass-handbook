## Аннотация (оригинал)

We combine the highest resolution N-body simulation of a $\sim 10^{12}\, M_\odot$ $Λ$CDM halo (Aquarius-A) with the {\sc GALFORM} galaxy formation semianalytic model to study the full satellite population expected in a MW-like galaxy.
